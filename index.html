ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

At KEDHAR Tours & Travels , we believe that every journey is a story waiting to be told. Whether you're an intrepid explorer seeking the thrill of adventure, a romantic couple dreaming of a secluded getaway, a family longing for quality bonding time, or a business traveler in pursuit of success, we are here to make your travel dreams a reality.
At the heart of KEDHAR Tours & Travels is a team of passionate globetrotters, seasoned explorers, and meticulous travel experts. With 65 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for delivering exceptional travel experiences tailored to your preferences and needs.

C. Ramadas: A Guiding Light in Pilgrim Tourism

C. Ramadas, the illustrious Founder and CEO of Kedhar Tours And Travels, is a luminary in the realm of pilgrim tourism, hailed for his unwavering commitment to providing transformative journeys for spiritual seekers across India.

Born into a lineage steeped in pilgrimage tradition, Ramadas inherited the legacy of his father, Dr. KVC Narayanan Nair, and embarked on a lifelong journey to illuminate the path to sacred destinations. Ramadas's pilgrimage odyssey began at the tender age of 16, as he followed in his father's footsteps, traversing the length and breadth of India to explore revered sites such as Kashi, Kedarnath, and the Great Kailas. With each pilgrimage, he deepened his understanding of the spiritual significance of these places, laying the foundation for his future endeavors.

Drawing inspiration from his father's pioneering spirit, Ramadas established Kedhar Tours and Travels, a testament to his vision of providing pilgrims with enriching and authentic experiences. Under his guidance, the company has flourished, earning acclaim for its meticulous planning, personalized service, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. The testimonials of countless happy customers bear testimony to the profound impact of Ramadas's guided tours.

From spiritual awakening to cultural immersion, travelers recount stories of transformation and renewal, crediting Ramadas for orchestrating journeys that transcend the physical realm and touch the soul. In the ever-evolving landscape of Bharat's pilgrim tourism, Ramadas's contributions loom large. As India witnesses significant milestones such as the construction of the Ayodhya Ram Temple and the resurgence of Sanatan Dharma, Ramadas stands as a stalwart guardian of tradition and heritage. His dedication to preserving India's sacred sites and promoting the country's rich spiritual legacy has earned him reverence and admiration from pilgrims far and wide.

A distinctive aspect of Kedhar Tours and Travels is its provision of Kerala-style cuisine during trips, prepared by skilled cooks from Kerala. This unique offering adds a touch of home and comfort, enhancing the overall pilgrimage experience for travelers. At the helm of Kedhar Tours and Travels, Ramadas is assisted by his son, Kailas R Das, who shares his father's passion for pilgrimage and commitment to excellence. Together with a team of experienced guides and culinary artisans, they continue to lead travelers on transformative journeys that leave an indelible mark on the soul.

In the luminous tapestry of India's pilgrim tourism, C. Ramadas shines brightly as a guiding light, illuminating the path to spiritual fulfillment and cultural enlightenment for generations to come.
